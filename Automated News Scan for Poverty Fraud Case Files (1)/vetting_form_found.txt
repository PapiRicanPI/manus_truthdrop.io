VETTING APPLICATION FORM FOUND!
================================

URL: https://truthdrop.io/apply

This is the public-facing vetting application form where users can apply for access to The Vault Investigates database.

Form Fields:
1. Full Name * (required)
2. Email Address * (required) - Professional/organizational emails prioritized
3. Organization / Affiliation (optional)
4. Your Role / Position (optional)
5. Reason for Requesting Access * (required) - Minimum 100 characters
6. How Will You Use This Data? * (required) - Minimum 100 characters
7. Terms and Conditions checkbox * (required)

Vetting Guidelines Displayed:
- ✓ Legitimate Purpose
- ✓ Professional Affiliation
- ✓ Responsible Use Commitment
- ✓ Verifiable Identity

Warning: "Applications may be rejected if they lack sufficient detail, demonstrate no clear purpose, or raise concerns about potential misuse."

Terms Applicants Must Agree To:
- Use data responsibly and ethically
- Properly cite The Vault Investigates as a source
- Not misrepresent or manipulate findings
- Not redistribute raw data without permission
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- Accept that access may be revoked for violations

This form submits applications to the admin vetting page at /admin/vetting for review.
